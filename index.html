Our Journey
The Englisho Story
Built in Maharashtra, for India — because everyone deserves to speak English confidently.
🔭 Our Vision
English for Every Indian
To make English fluency accessible to every Indian, regardless of their location, income or background — using technology they already use every day: WhatsApp.
🎯 Our Mission
Teach Without Shame
To deliver an English learning experience that feels like chatting with a supportive friend — no judgment, no red marks, no fear. Just confidence, one conversation at a time.

Crores of Indians know English words but fear speaking. They worry about making mistakes, being judged, or sounding wrong. Traditional coaching is expensive, time-consuming and often shameful.

We believed there was a better way. WhatsApp is already on every Indian phone. Why not use it to learn? Englisho was built on that simple idea — bring a world-class AI English tutor right into the app everyone already loves.

We believe real progress comes from daily practice, cultural understanding and gentle feedback. That's why we built a system where learners start where they are, guided by AI and supported every step of the way.

Need Help?
Frequently Asked Questions
🔍
Is Englisho really free? ▼
Yes! You can start completely free. Send a message to our WhatsApp number, take the placement test and get your first 20 messages free. After that, continue with just ₹49/month — less than a chai every week.
Do I need to download any app? ▼
No app needed at all! Englisho works entirely on WhatsApp, which you already have. Just save our number and start chatting — that's it.
Which languages are supported? ▼
Currently we support Marathi, Hindi, Tamil, Telugu, Gujarati, Punjabi, Kannada and Bengali. More languages are being added regularly. Tell us your language if it's not listed!
How is this different from Duolingo or other apps? ▼
Duolingo gives you exercises. Englisho gives you real conversation. Our AI tutor chats with you like a friend — about your life, your work, your day. And it explains things in your mother tongue, which no English app does. Plus, it works on WhatsApp so there's nothing to install or remember to open.
What is the placement test? ▼
It's a free 5-minute test with 14 MCQ questions + 1 speaking section + 1 writing section. It detects your English level from A1 (beginner) to C1 (advanced). After the test, your tutor automatically teaches at the right difficulty for you.
How much time do I need every day? ▼
Just 15 minutes! Your tutor sends a new topic every day. You can reply whenever you have time — morning commute, lunch break, before bed. Consistency matters more than long sessions.
Can I use voice instead of typing? ▼
Yes! You can send voice notes on WhatsApp and your tutor will respond to what you said. This is great for practicing pronunciation and building speaking confidence.
